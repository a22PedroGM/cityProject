People Born

Marrakech or Marrakeshnota is one of the most important cities in Morocco.
It has 1,545,541 inhabitants and is located at the foot of the Atlas Mountains, at 466 meters above sea level. n. m. Tall.
It has numerous World Heritage monuments.
It is, together with Mequinez, Fez and Rabat, one of the four imperial cities of Morocco.
It was founded in 1062 by the Almoravids and was the capital of the Almoravid and Almohad Empire.
The city has the largest traditional market (suq) in the country and one of the busiest squares in Africa and the world, Djemaa el Fna. Acrobats, storytellers, water vendors, dancers and musicians gather in the square.
At night, the square fills with food stalls, turning into a large open-air restaurant.
